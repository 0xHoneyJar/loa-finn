```markdown

[2-3 sentences capturing the most important takeaway. What happened, why it matters, what to do next.]

[1-2 paragraphs connecting technical changes to business outcomes. Include metrics where available.]

## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| [Risk description] | Low/Medium/High | Low/Medium/High | [Action to reduce risk] |

## Recommended Actions

1. [Action item with owner and timeline]
2. [Action item with owner and timeline]
3. [Action item with owner and timeline]
```

## Constraints

- Executive Summary must not exceed 3 sentences
- Key Points limited to 7 items maximum
- Total document should be readable in under 3 minutes
- All technical terms must be explained inline on first use
